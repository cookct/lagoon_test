# THE LORE OF THE FIRST ONES: Narrative Outline
(32 Chapters, 5 Parts)

## Theme: The foundational sin of the wizarding world and the reclamation of an ancient species.

### Key Narrative Pillars:
1. **The Genetic Argument:** Magic is a heritable trait from First One/Mortal bonds.
2. **The Resonance Lock:** House-elves cannot harm wizards because all wizards are their literal descendants.
3. **The Old Tongue:** A language that requires internal emotional alignment to speak correctly.
4. **The First Mother:** The ancient ancestor of all magic, bound in a house-elf body for millennia.
