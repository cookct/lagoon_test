# MARAUDERS ERA LORE DATABASE

## CHARACTER PROFILES

### JAMES POTTER (Prongs)
- **Born**: March 27, 1960
- **Blood Status**: Pure-blood
- **Parents**: Fleamont and Euphemia Potter (elderly, wealthy, died of Dragon Pox shortly after his wedding)
- **Upbringing**: Only child, pampered and spoiled by loving parents; never wanted for anything
- **Hogwarts**: Gryffindor, 1971-1978; Chaser on Quidditch team
- **Animagus**: Stag (achieved 5th year, illegal and unregistered)
- **Personality**: Confident, arrogant (softening in 6th-7th year), clever, loyal, natural leader
- **Key Relationships**: 
  - Best friend: Sirius Black (brothers in all but blood)
  - Love interest: Lily Evans (pursuing; she dislikes his arrogance)
  - Rival: Severus Snape (bullying dynamic, saved his life in 5th year)
- **Notable**: Inherited Invisibility Cloak (Deathly Hallow) from father; family wealthy from Sleekeazy's Hair Potion

### SIRIUS BLACK (Padfoot)
- **Born**: November 3, 1959
- **Blood Status**: Pure-blood (disowned)
- **Parents**: Orion Black (d. 1979) and Walburga Black (d. 1985)
- **Brother**: Regulus Black (1961-1979, Death Eater, died destroying Horcrux)
- **Home**: 12 Grimmauld Place, London - oppressive, dark, full of Dark artifacts
- **Upbringing**: Abusive household; emotional/verbal abuse from parents; ran away at 16 (summer 1976)
- **Ran Away To**: The Potters - treated like a second son
- **Inheritance**: Uncle Alphard left him money (Alphard's name blasted off tapestry)
- **Animagus**: Large black dog (achieved 5th year)
- **Personality**: Reckless, charismatic, fiercely loyal, dark humor, hiding trauma
- **Key Relationships**:
  - James: Brothers in all but blood
  - Remus: Complicated by "the Prank" (trust issues)
  - Regulus: Estranged; Sirius despises his Death Eater path
  - Snape: Mutual hatred; the Prank nearly got Snape killed

### REMUS LUPIN (Moony)
- **Born**: March 10, 1960
- **Blood Status**: Half-blood (Muggle mother)
- **Parents**: Lyall Lupin (Ministry expert on Dark creatures) and Hope Howell (Welsh Muggle, d. before 1981)
- **Condition**: Werewolf since age 4 - bitten by Fenrir Greyback as revenge for Lyall's insult
- **Upbringing**: Isolated, moved frequently, loving but worried parents
- **Hogwarts**: Gryffindor, Prefect; Dumbledore arranged Shrieking Shack for transformations
- **Financial Status**: Family struggled; Remus faces lifelong employment discrimination
- **Personality**: Quiet, observant, dry wit, self-deprecating, grateful for friends' acceptance
- **Key Relationships**:
  - The Marauders: His only real friends; they became Animagi for him
  - Sirius: Trust damaged by the Prank; slowly rebuilding
- **Notable**: Uses "visiting sick mother" as excuse for full moon absences

### PETER PETTIGREW (Wormtail)
- **Born**: Around 1960
- **Blood Status**: Unknown (likely pure or half-blood)
- **Mother**: Mrs. Pettigrew (received Order of Merlin after his "death")
- **Father**: Unknown/absent
- **Animagus**: Rat (achieved 5th year)
- **Personality**: Weak, eager to please, worships his friends, the tagalong
- **Role**: The question of what turns a friend into a traitor haunts the era
- **Notable**: Received Order of Merlin, First Class (posthumously, faked); only a finger recovered

### LILY EVANS
- **Born**: January 30, 1960
- **Blood Status**: Muggle-born
- **Parents**: Muggles (killed by Voldemort between 1978-1981)
- **Sister**: Petunia Dursley (estranged, jealous, married to Vernon)
- **Hogwarts**: Gryffindor, Prefect, Head Girl; brilliant at Potions
- **Personality**: Intelligent, principled, sharp-tongued, not impressed by arrogance
- **Relationship with James**: Dislikes his bullying; friendship develops 7th year; dating by graduation
- **Friendship with Snape**: Childhood friends; ended when he called her "Mudblood" and joined Death Eaters

### SEVERUS SNAPE
- **Born**: January 9, 1960
- **Blood Status**: Half-blood (Prince family on mother's side)
- **Parents**: Tobias Snape (Muggle, abusive) and Eileen Prince (witch)
- **Home**: Spinner's End, Cokeworth - poverty, unhappy household
- **Hogwarts**: Slytherin, 1971-1978; brilliant at Potions, created spells
- **Personality**: Bitter, precise, intelligent, vindictive, obsessive
- **Key Relationships**:
  - Lily Evans: Childhood best friend; in love with her; destroyed friendship by calling her "Mudblood"
  - James Potter: Sworn enemy; bullied by Marauders; saved by James in 5th year (owes life debt)
  - Sirius Black: Mutual hatred; nearly killed by Remus in the Prank
- **Path**: Joins Death Eaters after Hogwarts; eventually becomes double agent

### REGULUS BLACK
- **Born**: 1961
- **Hogwarts**: Slytherin (5th year in 1976-1977)
- **Path**: Death Eater (joined at 16); becomes disillusioned
- **Death**: 1979 - drowned by Inferi after switching Horcrux locket; Kreacher witnessed
- **Relationship with Sirius**: Estranged; Sirius thinks he's just another Death Eater

## TIMELINE: 1976-1977 (SIXTH YEAR)

### Summer 1976
- Sirius runs away from Grimmauld Place at 16
- Walburga blasts his name off the family tapestry
- Sirius moves in with the Potters
- The Prank (Snape/Shrieking Shack incident) - occurred recently, still raw

### September 1976 - Start of Sixth Year
- NEWT-level classes begin
- Quidditch season approaches
- Full moon transformations continue
- Rising Death Eater activity in the wizarding world

### Key Events of the Era
- **The Prank**: Sirius told Snape how to get into the Shrieking Shack during a full moon. James saved Snape at the last moment. Snape saw Remus as a werewolf. Dumbledore swore Snape to secrecy. Sirius and Remus's friendship damaged.
- **The Map**: Marauder's Map either completed or near completion
- **Animagi**: All three (James, Sirius, Peter) achieved transformation by 5th year
- **Werewolf Accommodations**: Remus uses Whomping Willow tunnel to Shrieking Shack each full moon

## LOCATIONS

### Hogwarts Castle
- **Gryffindor Tower**: Marauders' dormitory, common room
- **Great Hall**: Meals, mail, important announcements
- **Dungeons**: Potions classroom (Snape's domain), Slytherin territory
- **Astronomy Tower**: Late-night meetings, astronomy classes
- **Room of Requirement**: May have been discovered (canon unclear)
- **Whomping Willow**: Planted specifically for Remus; tunnel to Shrieking Shack

### Hogsmeade
- **Three Broomsticks**: Popular gathering spot
- **Honeydukes**: Sweets, secret passage to Hogwarts
- **Zonko's**: Joke shop (Marauder favorite)
- **Shrieking Shack**: Remus's monthly prison; supposedly haunted

### Grimmauld Place (12)
- Black family home; dark, oppressive, full of Dark artifacts
- House-elf Kreacher; Walburga's screaming portrait
- Sirius escaped at 16; never returned willingly

### Potter Cottage (Godric's Hollow)
- James's family home; welcoming, warm
- Sirius's refuge after running away
- Fleamont and Euphemia died shortly after James's wedding (1978-1979)

## THE WIZARDING WORLD: 1976-1977

### Political Climate
- Voldemort at height of power
- First Wizarding War ongoing
- Disappearances and murders common
- Ministry struggling to contain the threat
- Order of the Phoenix operating secretly (founded ~1970)

### Blood Prejudice
- Pure-blood supremacy ideology driving Death Eaters
- Muggle-borns targeted
- "Mudblood" slur common among Slytherins
- The Black family motto: "Toujours pur" (Always pure)

### After Graduation (1978)
- All four Marauders join the Order of the Phoenix
- James and Lily marry (1978-1979)
- Harry born July 31, 1980
- Peter becomes Secret Keeper; betrays Potters (October 31, 1981)
- James and Lily killed; Sirius framed for Peter's "murder"
- Only Remus survives (of the four friends)

## MAGICAL DETAILS

### Animagus Transformation
- Extremely difficult magic; usually takes years
- Illegal to be unregistered (Azkaban sentence)
- Requires holding mandrake leaf in mouth for a month
- Each Marauder's form reflects inner nature:
  - James: Stag (noble, leader)
  - Sirius: Dog (loyal, fierce)
  - Peter: Rat (small, overlooked, survivor)

### The Marauder's Map
- Created using advanced Homonculous Charm
- Shows everyone in Hogwarts in real time
- Nicknames: Moony, Wormtail, Padfoot, Prongs
- Insult: "Mr. Moony presents his compliments to Professor Snape, and begs him to keep his abnormally large nose out of other people's business."
- Confiscated by Filch in later years; found by Fred and George Weasley

### Werewolf Lore
- Transformation every full moon
- No cure exists
- Wolfsbane Potion not invented until 1990s
- Werewolves feared and discriminated against
- Fenrir Greyback: targets children, aligned with Voldemort

## DIALOGUE VOICES

### James
- Confident, teasing, occasionally arrogant
- Uses nicknames ("Snivellus" for Snape)
- Quick with a quip
- Softens when talking about Lily

### Sirius
- Dramatic, theatrical flair
- Sarcastic, dark humor
- Can be cruel when angry
- Uses pure-blood elegance as weapon or shield

### Remus
- Quiet, measured
- Dry, self-deprecating wit
- Chooses words carefully
- Often the voice of reason

### Peter
- Eager to please
- States the obvious
- Nervous laughter
- Worships his friends

### Lily
- Sharp, intelligent
- Not easily impressed
- Principled (calls out James's bullying)
- Warm with those she cares about

### Snape
- Bitter, precise
- Formal even when cruel
- Acidic sarcasm
- Never forgets a slight

## WRITING NOTES

### Era Themes
- **Friendship**: The bond between the Marauders is the heart of the era
- **Loyalty vs. Betrayal**: Peter's future hangs over everything
- **Coming of Age**: Young people making choices that define their lives
- **War**: Darkness encroaching on childhood
- **Class and Blood**: Pure-blood mania vs. Muggle-born acceptance
- **Trauma**: Sirius's abuse, Remus's condition, Snape's poverty

### Emotional Undertones
- Joy shadowed by coming darkness
- Every laugh has an edge
- The reader knows how it ends; the characters don't
- Bittersweet nostalgia
- The last golden days before everything falls apart

### Common Tropes (Fanon)
- **Wolfstar**: Sirius/Remus romantic pairing
- **Jily**: James/Lily slow burn
- **Prank Aftermath**: Trust issues between Sirius and Remus
- **Running Away**: Sirius's escape from Grimmauld Place
- **Full Moon Nights**: The Marauders roaming as animals
- **Detention**: Frequent punishment with Filch
- **Quidditch**: James's passion and talent
